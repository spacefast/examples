Why the tip sits on the pre-tax bill

1

Tax isn't a service

Sales tax goes to the state, not your server. Tipping on top of it quietly pads the bill. Even Split tips on the food & drink and leaves tax out of it.

2

Same math, every time

One pre-tax base, one percentage, one honest number. No "wait, did we tip on the total?" Pass the phone and everyone sees exactly how it was figured.

3

Round up if you like

Flip on round-up and each person pays a clean dollar amount. The few cents of cushion land on the tip — your server won't mind.
