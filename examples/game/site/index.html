How to play

  1. Move the paddle with the ← → arrow keys, A/D, your mouse, or by dragging on touch.
  2. Launch the ball with Space or a tap. The angle depends on where the ball sits on the paddle.
  3. Smash every brick to clear the level. Tougher bricks take two or three hits — watch them crack.
  4. Catch falling power-ups with your paddle:
  • M Multi-ball — splits into three.
  • W Wide paddle — bigger catch zone.
  • S Slow-mo — eases the ball speed.
  • L Laser — Space fires bolts.
  • + Extra life — a rare gift.

Five levels, escalating speed. Don't let the ball fall past your paddle — you've got three lives.
