So… what actually is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a weekend-long build sprint. You team up (or fly solo), pick something you wish existed, and make a working version of it in 36 hours. There are workshops if you're stuck, mentors who've shipped real software, and a science-fair-style expo at the end where everyone demos what they made. No exams. No grades. Just building.

🌱

Beginners absolutely welcome

About 4 in 10 RamHackers have never been to one before. We run a "Hack 101" track, beginner-friendly workshops, and roaming mentors so your first commit isn't your last.

👥

Teams of 1–4

Bring friends or make them at Saturday's team-formation mixer. Solo hackers are totally fine too — plenty of people build alone and crush it.

🎓

Open to all students

Any enrolled college student (or a recent grad, within a year) 18+, any major. CS, design, business, biology — the best teams mix it up.

🆓

Completely free

Zero entry fee. We feed you six meals, caffeinate you relentlessly, hand out swag, and cover travel for teams coming from out of town.
