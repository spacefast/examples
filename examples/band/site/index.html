Who we are

About the band

The Tidal Wolves started as four friends trading songs on a leaky houseboat in Portland. What came out was loud-quiet folk built for big rooms and last-call bars alike — close-harmony vocals over banjo, cello, and a kick drum that sounds like the tide pulling out.

Low Tide Cathedral, their second record, was tracked live to two-inch tape in a converted dairy barn near the coast. You can hear the rain on the roof in “Ferry Lights.” We left it in.

  • Della Marsh — vocals, acoustic guitar
  • Cormac Vane — banjo, mandolin, harmonies
  • Ines Okafor — cello, keys
  • Søren Vale — drums, percussion
