The studio

Small on purpose.

We started Northbound in 2017 because the best work we'd ever made came from tiny teams and short distances between idea and decision. We've kept it that way. When you hire us, you work with the people doing the work — no account layer, no hand-offs to a junior, no surprises.

We believe a brand is a promise you can see. Our job is to make that promise clear, distinctive, and durable — then build the website that delivers it.
